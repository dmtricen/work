### What you need
1. You need to order the RKL from Verifone (for our purposes, we have the office order it).  Supply them with the Serial Number of the pinpad and the Brand of the store (BP, Exxon, etc.)
2. Computer with internet access
3. USB drive


---
### The Proceedure

1. Wait for the email that informs you that the RKL has been ordered and being created.  
2. Once you receive it, open the email and note the order number (starts with WEB-)
3. Go to the Verifone premier site and login
4. Under View > VeriShield Remote Key, select Recent VRK Requests
5. Find the order number and click it 
6. Scroll down to the box titled “Search Key Files to Download” and click the Download button
7. It will pop up a window and immediately download the RKL without you clicking anything. 
8. Once it’s downloaded, copy the file over to your USB drive. 
9. Connect the USB drive to the Pinpad
	1. Connect the USB drive to the usb dongle THEN connect that to the Pinpad power brick in the usb port
	2. ![USBDongle.jpeg](/img/user/Assets/Images/USBDongle.jpeg)
10. Now, follow the procedure in the manual [[M400PassportGuide.pdf]] on page 37.
